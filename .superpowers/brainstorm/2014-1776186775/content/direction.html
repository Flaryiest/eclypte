How should the post-hero sections feel?

The hero is atmospheric (parallax image, orbs, particles, grain). After it, the page drops to flat #000. Which direction should we take to bridge that?

A — Continued atmosphere

Keep faint orbs, particles, and grain drifting through the whole page so the hero never really "ends." Most cohesive, highest effort.

B — Deep gradient backdrop

Shift from pure black into a very dark tinted gradient (warm gold or cool purple), with a soft bloom near section breaks. Cheap, subtle.

C — Spotlight glows per section

Large, soft radial glows behind each section heading — like stage lighting. Black stays black, but each section has a presence.

D — Texture: hairline grid + grain

A very faint editorial grid and persistent film grain give the surface "tooth" without color. Feels like print/editorial design.

These aren't exclusive — we can combine (e.g., B+C). Pick the one closest to the vibe you want, or tell me in the terminal.
